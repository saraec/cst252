About Me

I am a third year transfer at CSUMB. I am majoring in Communication Design with a concentration in visual design. My favorite hobbies and pass times include watching movies & T.V., painting, and reading occasionally. My favorite films and shows are Killing Eve, any 90's movie with Sandra Bullock, Juno, and Bones. I recently finished a book titled Her Body and Other Parties by Carmen Maria Machado which is a collection of body horror short stories. Now, I am reading Girl, Interrupted after I watched the movie and found out it was based on a true story. I live in Hollister, but I spend a lot of my time in the surrounding towns. On my days off I like to come to Monterey or go to Santa Cruz, get coffee, shop, get lunch, and go to the beach.
