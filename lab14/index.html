Lab 14: Loops

Subject

The subject of this lab is to use a loop to perform some kind of event.

Difficulties

The majority of the lab went very easily because we went over it in class. The for loop was understandable, creating the index and the 'if' conditions went well. I had a bit of trouble understanding how to get the output to print using a conditional.

Results

I was able to create a loop that differentiates numbers between 1-200 by multiples of 3, 5, and/or 7 by printing the words Fizz, Buzz, and/or Boom.
